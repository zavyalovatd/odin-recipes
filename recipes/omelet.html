Omelet

Homepage

Description

Why don't we look into some healthy morning food options with this oven baked omelet.

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 9 large eggs
  • half a cup of sour cream
  • half a cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • a quater cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish with butter.
  2. Beat eggs, sour cream, milk, and salt in a bowl until homogeneous. Stir in green onions. Pour mixture in the prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until set, 25 to 30 minutes. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over eggs and continue baking until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes more.

Bon appetit!
